hello Github community,<br />
here is one of my social application I have done it,<br />
There are amount of features in this app will let you to contact with your friend:<br />

User Features:

- Login & create new account in this app.<br />
- You can Upload your profile image & cover and you can change it in another time.<br />
- create a collections of posts and share it with your community including you can upload an images.<br />
- you can set a Comments & Likes on any posts you would like to give your opinions on it. <br />
- open a real time chat with your friend on app and exchange of messages and photo.<br />

Technical Features:

- The code completely follow the roles of clean code <br />
- The performance of the application has received attention so i build the app to edit the data by locally that would shift heavily in favor of the app and would increas the performance.<br />
- I used a firebase as a database, it gave me a huge features to handle a data & how to lead it in addition how to put it in right positions.<br />
- I gave this application a lot of my attention in terms of State Management in past i have used Getx & Riverpod as state management but at this point time i used to Provider & Bloc because these tools are more simple from the other.<br />
